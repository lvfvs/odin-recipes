Chilli Anchovy Pasta

Description

This dish is for when a 3PM hang turns into a dinner party or when you've had too long of a day at work.

But just because it's comfort food doesn't mean there aren't techniques. They're the same ones that go into every restaurant pasta you've ever loved: Salt the boiling water; cook the pasta al dente; create a silky sauce that marries pasta water, Parmesan, olive oil, and butter.

Ingredients

  • 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1⁄4 cup olive oil
  • 3 oil-packed anchovy fillets
  • 1⁄4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup parmesan, grated
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1⁄4 of a lemon
  • a generous amount of lemon zest
