Lemon Tuna Pasta

Description

An easy, delicious summer pasta made with simple pantry ingredients.

The pasta has a bright flavour, and is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner. It's a nostalgic recipe for me, since Dad used to cook it for us all the time. I've since adapted the recipe for my older, more experienced palette, and love it just as much as I always did.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can of tuna, drained (packed in oil)
  • 11⁄2 of a lemon
  • 2 tbsp reserved pasta water
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • a generous amount of lemon zest
  • parmesan cheese, shaved
  • fresh parsley to serve

Steps

  1. Boil a salted pot of water for your pasta and cook it al dente according to package directions. Prep your other ingredients while it cooks.
  2. When the pasta is close to being ready, add the oil to a small pan over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the garlic and cook it for 30 seconds.
  3. Stir in the tuna, lemon juice, and parsley. Let it heat through.
  4. Once the pasta is done, add the pasta water to the sauce and then drain the pasta and toss with the sauce. Season with salt & pepper as needed. Serve pasta with freshly grated parmesan cheese and lemon zest.
