Lemon Tuna Pasta

Description

An easy, delicious summer pasta made with simple pantry ingredients.

The pasta has a bright flavour, and is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner. It's a nostalgic recipe for me, since Dad used to cook it for us all the time. I've since adapted the recipe for my older, more experienced palette, and love it just as much as I always did.

ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can of tuna, drained (packed in oil)
  • 11⁄2 of a lemon
  • 1⁄2 cup reserved pasta water
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • a generous amount of lemon zest
  • parmesan cheese, shaved
  • fresh parsley to serve
